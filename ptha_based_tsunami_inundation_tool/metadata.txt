about=PTHA Based Tsunami Inundation Tool provides an automated workflow for mapping tsunami inundation zones using Probabilistic Tsunami Hazard Assessment (PTHA) derived water levels and high-resolution coastal elevation data. The tool integrates spatial analysis techniques including nearest-point association, water level computation at different exceedance rate, raster-based inundation detection, and polygon generation of hazard zones. It is intended to support tsunami hazard assessment, coastal risk analysis, and emergency planning.
